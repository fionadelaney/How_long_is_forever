GAME OF THE WEEK: Irrational Games BIOSHOCK 2007


"The question isn't who is going to let me; it's who is going to stop me?" Ayn Rand author of Atlas Shrugged (1957)

According to Bioshock, the answer is Big Daddy, a key battle character and guardian of the orphaned little sisters. Unless you can defeat him in Rapture, the toxic city beneath the waves, let's just say things just don't end well...



Ayn Rand's writings were a source of inspiration for the origins and demise of Rapture City in underwater-sci-fi, environmental-disaster game Bioshock.

Released in 2007 by Irrational Games, Bioshock enjoyed multiple incarnations with publisher K2 until it's most recent release for mobile in 2013.
